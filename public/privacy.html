Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Summary: ZodiacCycle collects health and personal data to provide cycle tracking and astrological insights. We take your privacy seriously, especially regarding sensitive health information. We do not sell your data. Ever.

1. Who We Are

ZodiacCycle ("we", "us", "our") is a menstrual cycle tracking and astrology application operated as an independent service. Our contact email is support@zodiaccycle.app.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide Directly

  • Account data: Email address, password (encrypted), name
  • Birth data: Date of birth, time of birth, place of birth — used solely to calculate your astrological chart
  • Health data: Menstrual cycle dates, flow intensity, symptoms, mood, discharge, sexual activity notes
  • Location data: Birth city coordinates and current city — used for astrological calculations and transit predictions
  • Payment data: Processed entirely by Stripe. We never store your card details. We store only your Stripe customer ID.

2.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Browser type and device type (for technical compatibility)
  • App usage patterns (which features you use, not what you enter)
  • Error logs for debugging purposes

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To calculate and display your astrological birth chart and daily transits
  • To track your menstrual cycle and predict future dates
  • To generate personalised AI insights via Claude (Anthropic)
  • To send you transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset)
  • To send cycle-related notifications if you have opted in
  • To process payments and manage your subscription via Stripe
  • To improve the app and fix bugs

4. Health Data — Special Category

Your menstrual and health data is classified as special category sensitive personal data under GDPR Article 9. We process this data only with your explicit consent, solely to provide the cycle tracking features you have requested. This data is never used for advertising, never sold, and never shared with third parties except as described in Section 5.

5. Who We Share Data With

We share data only with the following trusted service providers, strictly for operational purposes:

  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting (EU servers available)
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI predictions. We send anonymised astrological and cycle phase data. We do not send your name, email, or identifying information to Anthropic.
  • Stripe — payment processing. Subject to Stripe's own privacy policy.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Cloudflare — infrastructure and API routing
  • Netlify — web hosting
  • Google AdSense — advertising for free tier users only. Google may use cookies to serve relevant ads. Premium subscribers see no ads.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for marketing purposes.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to withdraw consent: Stop processing at any time by deleting your account
  • Right to object: Object to processing for certain purposes

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@zodiaccycle.app. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

  • Active account data is retained as long as your account exists
  • When you delete your account, all personal and health data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Payment records may be retained for up to 7 years for legal/tax compliance
  • Anonymised, non-identifiable analytics data may be retained indefinitely

8. Data Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store plain text passwords
  • Database access is restricted and authenticated
  • Payment data never touches our servers — processed directly by Stripe
  • We conduct regular security reviews

9. Cookies

ZodiacCycle uses minimal cookies:

  • Authentication cookies: To keep you logged in (essential, cannot be disabled)
  • Preference cookies: Language and notification settings (functional)
  • Google AdSense cookies: For serving ads to free tier users only (you can opt out via Google's ad settings)

10. Children's Privacy

ZodiacCycle is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately at support@zodiaccycle.app.

11. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States (Supabase, Anthropic, Stripe, Cloudflare). Where this occurs, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses where required under GDPR.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you by email and by displaying a notice in the app at least 14 days before material changes take effect. Continued use of ZodiacCycle after changes constitutes acceptance.

13. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights:
Email: support@zodiaccycle.app
Response time: within 30 days
