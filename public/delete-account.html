Delete Your ZodiacCycle Account

If you would like to delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the ZodiacCycle app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Tap "Delete Account"

Alternatively, you can request deletion via email:

📧 support@zodiaccycle.app

We will process your request within 7 days.

What data is deleted

  • Profile information
  • Cycle & health data
  • Notification preferences
  • All stored personal data

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
